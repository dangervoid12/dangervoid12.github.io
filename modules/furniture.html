Furniture Designer

Parametric Furniture & Cabinet Design

The Furniture Designer module in GcodePlanner allows for rapid creation of professional-grade cabinets, bookcases, and custom furniture. By using a parametric approach, you can adjust dimensions in real-time while the software automatically manages panels, hardware placement, and fabrication details.

Intelligent Features

  • Parametric Templates: Bookcases, base cabinets, wardrobes, desks, workbenches, and more.
  • Zoned Layouts: Split bays into multiple zones with independent doors, drawers, or open shelving.
  • Automatic Hardware: Intelligent placement of concealed hinges, drawer slides, and shelf pins based on industry standards.
  • Joinery Optimization: Automatic generation of carcass bolts, dowels, and dado joints for robust construction.
  • One-Click Export: Send all panels directly to the Nesting module or export as production-ready DXF files.
  • Dynamic Dragging: Interactively adjust shelf positions and zone splits directly in the 3D viewport.

Whether you are a professional cabinet maker or a hobbyist woodworker, the Furniture Designer streamlines your workflow from initial concept to the final G-code.

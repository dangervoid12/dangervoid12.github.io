CAD Drawing Module

Powerful Integrated 2D CAD Editor

The CAD Drawing Module in GcodePlanner provides a full-featured 2D drafting environment designed specifically for manufacturing. It eliminates the need for external software by providing professional tools to create, edit, and optimize DXF/DWG drawings for CNC production.

Precision CAD Drafting Interface

Advanced Geometry Editing & Snapping

Additional drawing workflow view

Key Features

  • Full DXF/DWG Support: Open, edit, and save industry-standard CAD files with high compatibility.
  • Advanced Snapping: Precise endpoint, midpoint, intersection, center, and tangent snapping for accurate geometry.
  • Geometric Constraints: Professional editing tools including offset, trim, extend, fillet, chamfer, and polar/rectangular arrays.
  • Layer Management: Organize complex drawings with customizable layers, colors, and line types.
  • Drawing Cleanup: Intelligent tools to remove duplicates, join broken segments, and optimize geometry for CAM.
  • Dimensioning & Text: Add professional annotations and dimensional callouts directly to your production drawings.

With the CAD Drawing module, GcodePlanner becomes a complete end-to-end solution, taking your ideas from a blank screen to high-precision CNC parts in one seamless workflow.
